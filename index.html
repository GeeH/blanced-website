About Blanced Health and Safety

Your Trusted Safety Partner

Blanced Health and Safety Consultancy is a registered company in England and Wales, dedicated to helping businesses across South Wales maintain the highest standards of workplace safety.

With over 30 years of experience in the health and safety industry, our NEBOSH qualified consultants bring a wealth of knowledge and practical expertise to every client engagement.

Based in Port Talbot, we serve businesses throughout South Wales, providing comprehensive health and safety solutions tailored to your specific needs.
